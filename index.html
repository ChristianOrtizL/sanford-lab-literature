About this site

Each paper has its own page with: big-picture summary, gaps in the field before the paper, key findings, experimental approach, granular details, implied next steps, and how direct RNA sequencing on the Oxford Nanopore PromethION could extend the work. Pathway diagrams show molecular relationships and link experiments to the claims they support.
